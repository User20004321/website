About Summer ILC

Welcome to the Summer International Learning Center (ILC)! Our program offers exciting opportunities for students to learn and grow during the summer months.

Whether you're interested in language immersion, cultural exchange, or academic enrichment, we have a wide range of courses and activities to suit your interests.
